## Usage Instructions

1. **Duplicate this template** for each new video project
2. **Fill in each section** with specific details for your video
3. **Add or remove scenes** as needed for your content
4. **Use the checklist** to track production progress
5. **Share with team members** for collaboration

## Tips for Video Game Content

- **Show gameplay early** to hook viewers interested in the game
- **Balance explanation with demonstration** - don't just talk, show the concepts
- **Use timestamps** for longer videos to help viewers navigate
- **Include on-screen text** for key concepts and terminology
- **Consider your audience's knowledge level** - explain terms that may be unfamiliar
- **Capture high-quality footage** at the resolution you'll export
- **Plan B-roll** for talking head segments
- **Script transitions** between different game concepts or examples
